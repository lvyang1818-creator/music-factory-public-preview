# Artist Generation Prompt Knowledge

## Purpose

Artist generation prompts must create coherent artist systems, not random personas.

## Artist Prompt Fields

- Identity.
- Realm.
- Voice.
- Philosophy.
- Music system.
- Visual language.
- Album logic.
- Works direction.
- Forbidden elements.

## Rules

- Artist must belong to a world.
- World must shape music and visuals.
- Voice must have cultural direction.
- Prompt output must remain editable by the Creative Director.
- Do not invent official content without approval.

## Agent Rule

Generated artist material is draft knowledge, not permanent truth, until approved and written into Brand Repository.
