# Headline Guidelines

## Purpose

Headlines create first memory. They must be short, direct, and atmospheric.

## Headline Rules

- Name the thing clearly.
- Avoid generic marketing claims.
- Avoid long explanatory sentences.
- Use rhythm and restraint.
- Let subcopy carry explanation.

## Good Directions

- Music Factory.
- Where Music Becomes Civilization.
- A House for Voices, Albums, Worlds and Archives.
- Archive as Memory.
- The Future of Artists.

## Forbidden Directions

- The ultimate AI music platform.
- Create songs faster with AI.
- Discover next-generation music tools.
- Build your music workflow.

## Agent Rule

If a headline could appear on a SaaS homepage, it is probably wrong for Music Factory.
