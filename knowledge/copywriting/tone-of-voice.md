# Tone of Voice

## Voice Direction

Music Factory copy should sound editorial, human, contemporary, precise, and culturally aware.

## Tone Keywords

- Calm.
- Intelligent.
- Sparse.
- Poetic when needed.
- Concrete.
- Non-technical.
- Non-sales.

## Rules

- Say less, mean more.
- Avoid AI tool language.
- Avoid startup slogans.
- Avoid hype.
- Avoid empty luxury wording.
- Prefer strong nouns and clear images.

## Agent Rule

Before writing copy, ask whether the line sounds like a cultural brand or a software landing page.
