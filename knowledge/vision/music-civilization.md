# Music Civilization

## Definition

Music Civilization means that Music Factory treats music as an ecosystem of voices, worlds, images, albums, rituals, archives, and memory.

## Scope

Music Civilization includes:

- Artist worlds.
- Album systems.
- Sound archives.
- Visual languages.
- Narrative structures.
- Prompt systems.
- Future media extensions.

## Why It Matters

This vision prevents the project from becoming only a song list or artist gallery.

## Agent Rule

When uncertain, choose the option that strengthens the sense of civilization, continuity, and cultural memory.
