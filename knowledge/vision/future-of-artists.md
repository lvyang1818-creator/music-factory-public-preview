# Future of Artists

## Vision

The future Music Factory artist is not only a performer.

An artist may be a guardian, a realm, a voice system, a visual language, a prompt lineage, an album body, and an evolving archive.

## Artist System

Each artist should eventually include:

- Identity.
- Realm.
- Voice.
- Music system.
- Visual language.
- Album logic.
- Works.
- Archive.
- Prompt rules.

## Human Direction

AI-generated artists must still feel directed by human taste, cultural judgment, and creative responsibility.

## Agent Rule

Do not reduce artists to profile cards, avatars, or output generators.
