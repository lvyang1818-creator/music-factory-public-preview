# Simple Rebuild Brief

## Goal

Rebuild Music Factory as a simple Chinese music website.

The visitor should understand the site within 5 seconds and reach a playable song within 1 to 2 clicks.

## Homepage

Required sections:

1. What this site is.
2. Three AI singers.
3. Latest songs with audio players.
4. Albums.
5. Archive link.

The homepage should not feel like a product deck, dashboard, abstract map, or cinematic ceremony.

## Singer Pages

Each singer page must include:

- Singer name.
- Portrait or cover image.
- Short Chinese introduction.
- Music style.
- Representative songs.
- Visible audio players.
- Link back to homepage.

## Song Display

Every song item should show:

- Song title.
- Singer.
- Cover if available.
- Short introduction.
- Audio player or clear external play link.

## Navigation

Use simple Chinese labels:

- 首页
- 歌手
- 歌曲
- 专辑
- 档案

Avoid:

- Back to Civilization.
- Enter World.
- Explore Map.
- Living Atlas.
- Memory Space.

## Visual Direction

Simple, clean, warm, readable.

Allowed:

- Good spacing.
- Clear sections.
- Calm typography.
- Meaningful cover images.

Avoid:

- Huge cinematic hero that hides content.
- Heavy animation.
- Abstract civilization wording.
- Complicated page transitions.
- English-first UI.

## Success Standard

A normal visitor can:

1. Know what the website is.
2. Choose a singer.
3. Play a song.
4. Browse more songs.

Without needing to understand any internal system architecture.
