# Information Architecture

## Purpose

The website information architecture organizes Music Factory as a brand, universe, artist system, work system, and archive.

## Core Hierarchy

1. Brand.
2. Vision.
3. Realms.
4. Artists.
5. Albums.
6. Works.
7. Archive.

## Homepage Role

The homepage establishes identity, atmosphere, brand philosophy, realm entrances, latest releases, archive direction, and footer orientation.

## Realm Page Role

Realm pages provide world context, guardian identity, music works, philosophy, archive preview, and next realm navigation.

## Archive Role

Archive preserves memory and gives structure to works, years, types, albums, and artist systems.

## Agent Rule

Do not add new page structures without checking the information hierarchy.
