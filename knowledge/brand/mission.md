# Mission

## Mission Statement

Music Factory exists to build a cultural home for voices, albums, worlds, and archives.

## Core Mission

The mission is to treat music as civilization, not disposable content.

Music Factory organizes sound, image, story, artist identity, album memory, and future creative systems into one coherent cultural structure.

## What This Means

- Music is not only playback.
- Artists are not only profiles.
- Albums are not only releases.
- Archives are not only lists.
- AI is not only a tool.
- The website is not only a showcase.

## Agent Rule

When making decisions, prioritize cultural clarity, long-term memory, and brand coherence over short-term visual novelty.
