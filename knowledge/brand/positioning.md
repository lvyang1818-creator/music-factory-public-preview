# Positioning

## Brand Positioning

Music Factory is a house for voices, albums, worlds, and archives.

It is not a generic music platform, not a conventional label website, and not a SaaS product.

## Positioning Keywords

- Music Civilization
- Artist Universe
- Archive
- Editorial
- Museum
- Contemporary Culture
- Human + AI

## Public Perception Goal

Users should feel that Music Factory is an international music brand with a world view, not a template website.

## Agent Rule

Any page, copy, prompt, layout, or interaction that makes Music Factory feel like a startup product must be reconsidered.
