# Philosophy

## Brand Philosophy

Music Factory believes that sound can become world, memory, image, and cultural structure.

## Principles

- Emotion before function.
- World before module.
- Archive before feed.
- Artist identity before avatar.
- Album as artifact.
- Human direction before machine output.

## Human + AI

Music Factory uses AI as part of the creative system, but the brand must always feel authored, directed, and human.

## Agent Rule

Do not describe Music Factory as an AI tool. Describe it as a cultural music brand that can include AI-created artists, works, images, and systems.
