# 观无界 Worldview

## Worldview

观无界 belongs to Spirit Realm, a world about sound returning to silence.

## Core Belief

Sound can become a path toward stillness.

## Realm Logic

- Spirit Realm is quiet.
- Spirit Realm is restrained.
- Spirit Realm is not a game world.
- Spirit Realm is not a fantasy cultivation setting.
- Spirit Realm is not a generic religious website.

## Forbidden Interpretations

- Do not mix 观无界 with 十里音's Eastern philosophy catalogue.
- Do not assign 《无相》 to 观无界.
- Do not use 《心经》《道德经》《庄子》 as Spirit Realm works.
