# 观无界 Visual Language

## Visual System

观无界 visual language should be quiet, bright, sacred, and restrained.

## Color Direction

- Warm white.
- Soft light.
- Pale gold.
- Low-contrast gray.

## Image Direction

- Large whitespace.
- Human presence as part of the world.
- No decorative halo excess.
- No fantasy-game costume language.

## Motion Behavior

- Slow fade.
- Gentle reveal.
- Minimal movement.

## Forbidden Visuals

- Game fantasy.
- Cyber effects.
- Religious cliché.
- Heavy gold ornament.
