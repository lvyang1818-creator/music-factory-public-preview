# 梦梦 Visual Language

## Visual System

梦梦 visual language should feel natural, soft, intimate, and contemporary.

## Color Direction

- Warm white.
- Natural green-gray.
- Soft earth tones.
- Gentle light.

## Image Direction

- Nature as emotional environment.
- Human softness.
- Breath and movement.
- Avoid fantasy creature language.

## Motion Behavior

- Gentle fade.
- Soft reveal.
- Slow movement.

## Forbidden Visuals

- Buddhist scripture visuals.
- Daoist classic visuals.
- Fantasy game world.
- Tech nature dashboards.
