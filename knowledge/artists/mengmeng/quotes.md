# 梦梦 Quotes

## Representative Quotes

> 梦会替风记住声音。

> 柔软不是退让，是另一种生长。

> 自然不解释，它只是陪伴。

## Quote Rules

- Quotes should be soft but not childish.
- Quotes should feel natural and human.
- Quotes must not borrow religious or philosophical canon.

## Keywords

- Dream.
- Breath.
- Nature.
- Growth.
- Softness.
