# 十里音 Worldview

## Worldview

十里音 belongs to Future Realm.

Future Realm is not pure futurism. It is Eastern philosophy translated through contemporary sound, image, and popular music.

## Core Belief

Old questions can return through new sound.

## Realm Logic

- Future does not mean cold technology.
- Philosophy does not mean academic explanation.
- Pop does not mean shallow entertainment.
