# 十里音 Quotes

## Representative Quotes

> 旧问题，会在新的声音里醒来。

> 看见不是答案，放下才是。

> 未来不是远方，是回声改变了形状。

## Quote Rules

- Quotes may carry philosophical tension.
- Quotes should remain contemporary.
- Quotes must not become academic explanation.
