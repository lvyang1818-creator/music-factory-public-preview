# Motion System

## Purpose

Motion must make Music Factory feel alive without making the interface perform. The motion language is Slow, Elegant, Invisible, Reveal Based, Quiet, and Contemporary.

## Allowed Motion

- Fade.
- Mask.
- Reveal.
- Subtle move.
- Controlled blur.
- Very light parallax when meaningful.
- Smooth section entry.

## Motion Tokens

| Token | Value | Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `motion.fast` | `260ms cubic-bezier(.2,.8,.2,1)` | Small hover or focus. |
| `motion.base` | `560ms cubic-bezier(.2,.8,.2,1)` | Standard transition. |
| `motion.slow` | `1100ms cubic-bezier(.19,1,.22,1)` | Editorial reveal. |
| `motion.breath` | `20000ms ease-in-out` | Ambient breathing backgrounds. |

## Reveal Rules

- Reveal follows reading order.
- Reveal should not bounce.
- Reveal should not scale aggressively.
- Reveal may combine opacity, mask, blur, and small movement.

## Hover Rules

- Hover must stay under control.
- Links may underline.
- Cards may move slightly.
- Covers may lift or clarify.
- Buttons must not glow.

## Ambient Motion

- Ambient motion should be nearly invisible.
- Breathing light may be used when it supports material atmosphere.
- Do not add particles to make a page feel advanced.

## Forbidden

- Complex particles.
- HUD scans.
- Infinite decorative rotation.
- Exaggerated parallax.
- Glow hover.
- Bounce.
- Tech startup motion language.

## Review Checklist

- Is the motion felt more than noticed?
- Does the motion support reading?
- Does it stay quiet and premium?
- Does it avoid technology effects?
