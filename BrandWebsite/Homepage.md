# Homepage V2

## Milestone

Milestone 18 - Homepage V2.

## Positioning

Homepage = Civilization Entry Portal.

Music Factory homepage is not a landing page, product website, SaaS homepage, dashboard, product showcase, or streaming front page.

It is:

A Gateway to Music Civilization.

## Entry Identity

The first viewport must operate as an entrance ritual.

Required content:

- Music Factory.
- A Civilization of Voices.
- Enter Civilization.

The hero must feel minimal, quiet, museum-grade, editorial, and human-scale.

## Required Page Structure

### 1. Entrance Layer

Purpose:

Introduce Music Factory as a civilization identity before any product function appears.

Required language:

- Music Factory.
- A Civilization of Voices.
- Enter Civilization.

### 2. Civilization Preview

Purpose:

Present the three core systems as civilization entry nodes:

- Artists Universe.
- Sound Civilization Map.
- Archive / Works.

These are not product cards.

They are threshold nodes into the world system.

### 3. Narrative Layer

Purpose:

Explain Music Factory through civilization, archive, and voice language.

Required concepts:

- Voices become worlds.
- Works become chapters.
- Archive preserves memory.
- The map connects civilizations.

Forbidden language:

- Product features.
- SaaS benefits.
- Dashboard metrics.
- E-commerce modules.
- Product listing copy.

### 4. Entry Navigation

Purpose:

Offer clear routes into the living system:

- Enter Artists.
- Explore Sound Map.
- View Archive.
- Discover Works.

## Visual System

Required:

- Editorial layout.
- Museum grade composition.
- Large whitespace.
- Quiet luxury style.
- Human-scale typography.
- Existing Design System tokens and page rhythm.

Forbidden:

- SaaS UI.
- Dashboard UI.
- Card grid system.
- Product listing layout.
- Data visualization homepage.
- E-commerce module rhythm.

## Motion System

Allowed:

- Fade In.
- Mask Reveal.
- Slow Scroll Transition.
- Soft Zoom.
- Subtle Parallax.

Forbidden:

- Strong interaction animation.
- Game-like motion.
- Particle effects.
- High-frequency scroll effects.

## System Connection

Homepage must connect to:

- Artists Universe.
- Sound Civilization Map.
- Archive.
- Works.

The system must support:

Homepage -> System -> Return Homepage.

## Technical Constraint

Milestone 18 must not modify the Milestone 16 data structure.

It must not break Sound Civilization Map.

It must not restructure Knowledge Base.

It may update Homepage UI and necessary return navigation labels.

# Homepage V3

## Milestone

Milestone 18.1 - Homepage V3.

## Positioning

Homepage V3 = A Cinematic Portal to Music Civilization.

V2 made the system clear.

V3 makes the entrance immersive.

The visitor should not feel they are browsing a website.

The visitor should feel they are entering a world made of voices, memory, archive, and civilization.

## Entrance Cinematic Layer

The first viewport must remove ordinary website noise.

Required content:

- Music Factory.
- A Civilization of Voices.
- Enter Civilization.

Motion:

- 6 to 10 second progressive reveal.
- Slow fade in.
- Mask reveal.
- Breathing light.
- Soft zoom of 2 to 5 percent.

Forbidden:

- Navigation-dominant first screen.
- Product language.
- Dashboard language.
- Particle effects.
- High-frequency motion.

## Civilization Emergence

The second viewport must present three civilization nodes:

- Artists Universe.
- Sound Civilization Map.
- Archive / Works.

They are not cards.

They are floating civilization thresholds.

Each node may reveal more information on hover, but the interaction must remain slow, quiet, and editorial.

## Narrative Layer

Core text:

Music Factory is not a platform.

It is a Civilization of Voices.

Writing style:

- Short.
- Rhythmic.
- Poetic.
- No product copy.

## Entry Flow Layer

Required route order:

1. Enter Artists.
2. Explore Sound Map.
3. Discover Works.
4. Enter Archive.

The flow should feel linear, guided, and ceremonial.

It must not become a grid.

## System Connection

Homepage V3 must connect to:

- Artists Knowledge Base through Artists Universe.
- Sound Map System.
- Works Catalog.
- Archive Timeline.

## Success Standard

The homepage should communicate Music Factory as a world before it communicates any function.

# Homepage V3.1 Practical Restoration

## Reason

Visual System V3 made the entrance cinematic, but the public homepage must remain readable and useful for first-time visitors.

The homepage should keep the Sound Civilization Map as the top-level worldview signal while restoring the practical browsing structure from the earlier website.

## Corrected Structure

### 1. Sound Map Entrance

The first viewport must use Chinese as the primary language.

Required content:

- 音乐工厂.
- 声音文明地图.
- 一句话说明 Music Factory is a map of AI singers, works, albums, and archive memories.
- Clear entries into Sound Map, Artists, Works, and Archive.

English may remain only as secondary atmosphere.

### 2. Three Artist Entry

The middle homepage layer must show three clear singer entrances:

- 观无界.
- 十里音.
- 梦梦.

Each entrance needs a short Chinese positioning sentence and route to the artist page or works.

### 3. Songs and Works Access

The lower homepage layer must restore practical access:

- 最新歌曲.
- 推荐作品.
- 专辑入口.
- 档案入口.

The visitor must understand where to listen and where to browse.

## Visual Rule

Keep V3's editorial and museum-grade restraint, but do not let cinematic abstraction hide core actions.

Homepage V3.1 is:

Sound Map first.

Artist entry second.

Songs and archive access third.
